Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?
Unfortunately, games do not currently save. We are working on a backend fix to this. If your game does save after a session, then we guess that you got lucky! The best way to prevent save data loss is to not close out of the browser or tab.
Which games are compatible?
All games in our library are tested and compatible with the emulator. You can find a full list on the emulator's home page organized by category.
What should I do if a game won't load?
Try refreshing the page and selecting the game again. If the problem persists, please submit a support ticket with the game name, error code, and browser you're seeing/using.
Is my data private?
Play data is stored and disposed locally on your device. We don't collect or store personal data from your gameplay.
How long does it take to get support?
We aim to respond to all support tickets within 24-48 hours. Complex issues may take longer to resolve.
